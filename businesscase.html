Business Case & Benefits Realization

How the standards treat business justification, value delivery and tracking benefits

📘 PMBOK

PMBOK links projects to organizational value via a value delivery system and benefits management, encouraging measurement of outcomes beyond technical deliverables.

📗 PRINCE2

PRINCE2 makes the business case central — it must be continually validated, and stage decisions depend on whether the business justification remains sound.

📙 ISO

ISO emphasizes alignment with strategic objectives and requires mechanisms for monitoring whether intended benefits are achieved and sustained post-delivery.

✅ Similarities

  • All require clarity on why the project exists and how benefits will be measured.
  • Ongoing validation of value is emphasized rather than one-time approval.

⚖️ Differences

  • PRINCE2 makes the business case the gatekeeper for decisions; PMBOK embeds benefits in value delivery; ISO ties monitoring to organizational strategy and compliance.
