Scheduling & Time Management

Dependencies, milestones, critical path, iterative timelines

📘 PMBOK

PMBOK provides detailed scheduling processes including defining activities, sequencing, estimating durations, developing and controlling schedules using techniques like critical path and iteration planning.

📗 PRINCE2

PRINCE2 links schedules to product delivery. Stage and team plans define timeframes with milestones and tolerances; deviations are managed through exception processes.

📙 ISO

ISO defines scheduling as planning, sequencing, and controlling activities within life cycle phases, ensuring alignment with governance milestones and decision points.

✅ Similarities

  • All three emphasize clear sequencing, milestones, and control.
  • Schedules integrate with planning hierarchy and governance decisions.

⚖️ Differences

  • PMBOK gives detailed scheduling techniques; PRINCE2 focuses on stage-based control; ISO integrates scheduling with governance checkpoints.
