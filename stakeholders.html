Stakeholder Engagement & Communication

Identification, mapping, engagement strategies, and communication practices

📘 PMBOK

Stakeholder Management knowledge area: identification, mapping, engagement strategies, and communication planning/monitoring.

📗 PRINCE2

Communication Management Strategy defines flows. Stakeholder engagement tied to governance roles and responsibilities.

📙 ISO

ISO 21502 promotes early stakeholder identification, engagement planning, and communication aligned with organizational governance.

✅ Similarities

  • All highlight early stakeholder identification.
  • All stress clear, planned communication practices.

⚖️ Differences

  • PMBOK = detailed processes; PRINCE2 = embedded in governance; ISO = high-level principles.
