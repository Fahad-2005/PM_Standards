Team Management, Leadership & Culture

Team composition, leadership styles, and high-performance culture

📘 PMBOK

Focuses on team development, leadership styles, and high-performance culture. Promotes servant leadership and collaboration.

📗 PRINCE2

Roles are predefined (Project Board, Project Manager, Team Manager). Leadership is governance-driven; team culture less emphasized.

📙 ISO

ISO 21502 highlights role clarity, competent leadership, and promoting collaboration. Links project culture with organizational values.

✅ Similarities

  • All emphasize clear roles and responsibilities.
  • Leadership is critical to project success in all frameworks.

⚖️ Differences

  • PMBOK = flexible leadership/culture focus; PRINCE2 = formal role/governance focus; ISO = organizational alignment focus.
